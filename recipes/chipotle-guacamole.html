Chipotle Guacamole

Description

This is not a copycat; it's the genuine article! Chipotle revealed its guac recipe several years ago, so you can make the real thing at home!

It's just as delicious as you remember and fairly simple to make, but a food processor speeds things up significantly, if you're in a rush.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe Hass avocados
  • 2 tsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp cilantro (chopped)
  • ¼ cup red onion (diced)
  • ½ jalapeño, including seeds (diced)
  • ¼ tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Cut the avocados in half and remove pits carefully.
  2. Scoop the avocados into a bowl.
  3. Toss and coat with lime juice.
  4. Add the salt and mash until a smooth consistency is achieved.
    • Pro tip: To achieve that extra-smooth consistency with a few delightful chunks, use a large metal whisk, instead of a fork or spoon, to help break up the avocado halves.
  5. Fold in the remaining ingredients and mix.
  6. Taste the guac and adjust seasoning, if necessary.
