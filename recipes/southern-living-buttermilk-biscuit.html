Southern Living Buttermilk Biscuit

Description

These delicious, crispy-and-flaky-on-the-outside, soft-on-the-inside biscuits will knock your socks off and they're not too difficult to make, either.

With only 3 ingredients and a minimal amount of elbow grease, you, too, can be the star of the breakfast club!

Ingredients

  • 1 stick grated butter (frozen)
    • Yes, use a cheese grater to grate a stick of frozen butter; trust me on this.
  • 2½ cups White Lilly brand self-rising flour
  • 1 cup buttermilk

Steps

  1. Toss butter and flour together. Freeze for 10 min.
  2. Make a well (i.e., divot/crater) in center of butter/flour mixture. Pour buttermilk into well and stir/mix into dry ingredients until just combined (about 15 stirs).
  3. Turn out onto floured surface and roll flat/rectangular. Fold in half, turn 90°, and roll again. Repeat 4 times. Bake at 475° for 15 min.
